# MadeEA Command Center — Progress Update

**Status:** Live and improving · prepared for client review

---

## Where we are
The Command Center is **up, running, and being used** — a secure, branded web app where an Executive Assistant runs their whole day from one screen. Since the last update, we focused on the team's feedback: making every feature **easy to understand and faster to use, without training.**

## What's working today
- **Dashboard** — your day at a glance: tasks, meetings, emails, automations.
- **Task Manager** — drag-and-drop board, now with **checklists, due dates, repeating tasks, "blocked by" links, and ready-made templates** (onboard a client, weekly report, book travel, and more).
- **Client Vault** — full client profiles with photos (upload or link).
- **Communication Center** — inbox triage with one-click **AI-drafted replies**, and a short "how it works" guide built in.
- **AI Suite & Quick Actions** — every AI tool now has **guided forms, examples, and tips** so it's clear what to enter; results export to a clean **PDF**. You can also **save and reuse** your common prompts.
- **SOPs** — procedures run as **working checklists** you can pin to the screen while you work.
- **Automations** — run them on demand and see real results.
- **AI Assistant** — a 24/7 helper that now also **knows the team's SOPs**.

## New this round (from the feedback)
- **Clearer guidance everywhere** — examples, tooltips, and a *"How this page works"* card on every page.
- **Guided tour** — a first-time walkthrough, and you can **replay it any time** from Settings.
- **Quick search & launch** — press **⌘K / Ctrl-K** to jump to anything (clients, tasks, SOPs, pages) and pin favorites.
- **Smarter notifications** — overdue/upcoming deadlines plus your own **reminders and follow-ups**.
- **Works on mobile** — checked across phones; nothing breaks on a small screen.
- **Settings** — replay the tour and sign out.

## How sign-in works
The app is **invite-only** (an internal tool for the team, not a public site). New team members are added by an administrator — there's no public sign-up.
